About Dr. Peter McCellan

A native Missourian, Dr. McClellan grew up in Jefferson City. A standout soccer player, Dr. McClellan went on to play Division one soccer at Drury University with his twin brother majoring in Biology with a minor in Global studies. Upon Graduation, Dr. McClellan attended a one year advanced education in General Dentistry Program. While fighting cavities for America, Dr. McClellan gained valuable experience treating patients with oral facial pain and patients in need of complex cosmetic restorative care. Dr. McClellan is a scholar of the world famous Dawson Academy out of St. Petersburg, FL.
