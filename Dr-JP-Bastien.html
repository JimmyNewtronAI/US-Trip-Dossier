About Dr. J-P Bastien

Dr. Bastien received his dental degree in 2006 from McGill University, renowned as the “Harvard of Canada.” Following his graduation, he completed an extensive residency program at the V.A. Medical Center in Gainesville, FL, before establishing his cutting-edge practice equipped with the latest advancements in dental technology in Tallahassee. With a comprehensive background and training, Dr. Bastien is a well-rounded dentist with extensive experience in all phases of general dentistry, providing exceptional care to both adults and children. He has pursued numerous post-graduate courses, further honing his expertise in cosmetics, dental implants, Clear Aligners, and Botox. Dr. Bastien is fluent in both French and Creole, allowing him to serve a diverse patient population effectively. Dr. Bastien is an active member of several esteemed dental associations, including the American Dental Association (ADA) and the Florida Dental Association (FDA). He also holds significant roles within the local dental community, serving as a member of the Leon County Dental Association Board and the TCC Dental Hygiene Advisory Board in Tallahassee. His dedication extends beyond his practice, as Dr. Bastien is deeply involved in volunteer work through the WeCare Dental Network and the Children’s Medical Services of the Florida Department of Health. He regularly volunteers at the Veteran’s Stand Down, the Dentistry from the Heart, and the Mission of Mercy. Known for his caring and compassionate approach, Dr. Bastien values the strong relationships he builds with his patients, prioritizing their comfort and trust. Outside of his professional life, Dr. Bastien cherishes spending quality time with his two children, Olivia and Julian. He enjoys family outings, soccer, traveling, and movies, balancing his dedication to dentistry with his love for his family and hobbies.
